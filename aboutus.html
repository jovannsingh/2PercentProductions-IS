About - us

2 Percent Productions is a one-man full service creative agency founded in 2021.
The founder of 2 Percent Productions is Jovan Singh and the name of the agency is based on Sikhism – a religion. There are 2% people in the world who are Sikhs, and the founder Jovan Singh is one of them. The main goal of 2 Percent Productions is to make innovative products for brands that helps them to make a name for themselves in the market. 2 Percent offers services from making all sorts of videos like ads, music video's to making breakthrough creative designs.
